Our Journey

From Hostiva to Sovryx Tech

Our story began with Hostiva, a web development startup founded by Mohit Shrestha in Biratnagar, Nepal. Starting small with just a few passionate developers, we quickly gained recognition for our quality work and commitment to client success. As our expertise grew and demand increased, we evolved into Sovryx Tech — a comprehensive IT solutions company serving businesses across Nepal and beyond. Today, our 8+ member team continues to innovate and deliver exceptional digital solutions that drive real business results.
