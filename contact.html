FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Sovryx Tech.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A basic website takes 2-4 weeks, while complex software solutions may take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages starting from NPR 1,500/month for websites and NPR 3,000/month for software. This includes updates, security monitoring, and technical support.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in API integration and can work with your existing infrastructure. Whether it's connecting to your CRM, payment gateways, or other business systems, we ensure seamless integration.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, eSewa, Khalti, and other popular payment methods in Nepal. For international clients, we also accept PayPal and bank wire transfers.

Do you provide training for our team?

Yes, we include basic training with every project. For more comprehensive training, we offer IT training programs covering web development, programming, and practical IT skills.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer unlimited revisions during development and a 30-day post-launch support period. If you're not happy, we'll work with you until you are.
